How we protect your privacy and handle your data

Last Updated: January 1, 2025

Your Privacy Matters

At Datools, we are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring that your personal information remains secure. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect information when you use our calculator tools and website. Our privacy-first approach means we minimize data collection and prioritize user control over personal information.

Calculator Data

We do NOT collect calculator input data. All calculations are performed locally in your browser. When you enter financial information, test scores, or other data into our calculators, this information never leaves your device. We have no access to your calculation inputs or results.

Website Usage Information

We may collect minimal, anonymized information about how our website is used, including:

  • General geographic location (country/region level only)
  • Browser type and version for compatibility purposes
  • Pages visited and time spent (for improving user experience)
  • Referral sources to understand how users find our tools

Contact Information

When you contact us through email or contact forms, we collect only the information you voluntarily provide, such as your email address and message content. This information is used solely to respond to your inquiry and is not shared with third parties.

What We Don't Collect

  • Personal identification information (names, addresses, phone numbers)
  • Financial account information or payment details
  • Social security numbers or government identification
  • Detailed personal profiles or tracking across websites
  • Any calculator input values or calculation results

Client-Side Processing

Our calculators use client-side JavaScript, meaning all calculations happen in your browser rather than on our servers. This approach ensures that sensitive financial and personal information never leaves your device, providing maximum privacy protection.

Secure Communication

Our website uses HTTPS encryption to protect any communication between your browser and our servers. This ensures that any data transmitted (such as contact form submissions) is encrypted and secure from interception.

Minimal Data Storage

We store minimal information on our servers and automatically delete unnecessary data. Contact inquiries are retained only as long as needed to provide support and resolve issues.

No Third-Party Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or share personal information with third parties for marketing purposes. Any service providers we work with are bound by strict confidentiality agreements and data protection requirements.

Essential Cookies Only

We use minimal cookies necessary for website functionality. These may include:

  • Session cookies to maintain calculator state during your visit
  • Preference cookies to remember your settings (if implemented)

No Advertising Cookies

We do not use advertising cookies, tracking pixels, or other technologies designed to follow your browsing behavior across websites. Our tools are ad-free, and we don't engage in behavioral advertising.

If we use analytics tools, they are configured to respect user privacy with anonymized data collection and no personal identification. Users can opt out of analytics through browser settings or our privacy controls.

Since we collect minimal personal information, most data control happens automatically through our privacy-by-design approach. For any information we do have (such as contact inquiries), you can:

  • Request access to information we have about you
