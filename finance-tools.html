üè¢ Professional & Business Finance

DSCR Loan Calculator

The Debt Service Coverage Ratio (DSCR) calculator is essential for commercial real estate investors, business owners, and commercial lenders. This sophisticated tool calculates the ratio of net operating income to total debt service, determining loan qualification and risk assessment for various commercial lending scenarios.

Different loan types have varying DSCR requirements, and our calculator accounts for these differences, providing accurate qualification assessments for commercial real estate, investment properties, business loans, and SBA financing. This tool helps borrowers prepare loan applications and understand lender requirements.

Construction Loan Calculator

Construction financing involves unique complexities, and our calculator addresses the two-phase nature of construction-to-permanent loans. During the construction phase, borrowers typically pay interest-only on funds drawn, while the permanent phase converts to a traditional mortgage.

The tool calculates both phases comprehensively, helping builders, developers, and homeowners understand the full cost structure of construction projects. This includes interest-only payments during construction, final loan amounts after completion, and permanent mortgage payments post-construction.
