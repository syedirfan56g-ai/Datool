How to Use

  1. Enter your Monthly Gross Income from the property or business
  2. Input Monthly Operating Expenses (taxes, insurance, maintenance, etc.)
  3. Enter the Proposed Loan Amount you want to borrow
  4. Input the Interest Rate quoted by the lender
  5. Select the Loan Term (repayment period)
  6. Choose the Loan Type to determine minimum DSCR requirements
  7. Click Calculate DSCR to see if you qualify
  8. DSCR Formula: Net Operating Income ÷ Total Debt Service
  9. Minimum DSCR: Usually 1.20-1.35 depending on loan type
  10. Higher DSCR = Better qualification and potentially lower rates
