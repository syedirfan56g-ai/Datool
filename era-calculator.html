Earned Run Average

How to Use

  1. Enter the total number of Earned Runs allowed by the pitcher
  2. Enter the total Innings Pitched (can include partial innings like 6.2 for 6⅔ innings)
  3. Click Calculate ERA to get the result
  4. The ERA is calculated using the formula: (Earned Runs × 9) ÷ Innings Pitched
  5. A lower ERA indicates better pitching performance
