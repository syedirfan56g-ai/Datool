How to Use

  1. Enter the Total Project Cost for your construction
  2. Input your Down Payment amount
  3. Enter the Construction Interest Rate (typically variable rate)
  4. Select the Construction Term (how long to build)
  5. Enter the Permanent Mortgage Rate (rate after construction)
  6. Select the Permanent Term (long-term mortgage length)
  7. Click Calculate Construction Loan for results
  8. Note: During construction, you typically pay interest-only on funds drawn
  9. After construction, the loan converts to a permanent mortgage
