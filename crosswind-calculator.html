How to Use

  1. Enter the Wind Speed in knots
  2. Enter the Wind Direction in degrees (0-360, where 0° is North)
  3. Enter the Runway Heading in degrees (direction you're facing on the runway)
  4. Click Calculate Wind Components to get the results
  5. Crosswind: Wind component perpendicular to the runway (affects lateral control)
  6. Headwind: Wind component along the runway (positive = headwind, negative = tailwind)
  7. Use these values for safe landing and takeoff planning
